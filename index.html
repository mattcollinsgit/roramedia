The Story

Rora Media is a design and visual communication studio based in Glasgow. We help our clients express themselves and their products through bold brand identities and beautiful experiences.

Our craft strives to clarify purpose, build meaning, and stir up curiosity and feeling. Essentialy that's what makes us passionate and proud.

We endeavour to understand and build something fresh and exciting for people who equally want to flourish in a world where the internet can empower.

We love what we do and we know you do too.

Our Services

Web Design

The Internet is at the heart of Rora Media. We design and develop the most impressive, responsive websites online. Promoting your companies image and helping you interact with your customers like never before.

Graphic Design

From crafting a new logo to creating a new company image. We can help you achieve this in both digitally and in print. A unique image that will represent your company across all forms of media.

E-commerce

The digital marketplace! Bringing your products to the online world. Secure, beautiful and unique. We create the feel of a physical store online and available to millions.

Content Control

Know as a ‘content management system’, we make sure that you can interact with your customers. Booking systems, forums, whatever you need we can provide.

Content

Making sure your content not only shows what you can do but that it also represents your company perfectly. At Rora media we specialize in photography and videography that will bring your company to life.

Consultancy

Still unsure about what you need? How to go about a plan or how to execute your ideas? Rora Media provides simple, usable consultancy to help you better develop your digital presence.
